# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

AI Relay contributors are expected to be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- giving actionable feedback;
- respecting different levels of experience;
- focusing criticism on ideas and code, not people;
- protecting user privacy and security.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, or publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this standard. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the repository owner through GitHub.
